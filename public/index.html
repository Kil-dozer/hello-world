What is Rob's favorite hobby?

My Favorite hobby is both the Dungeons and the Dragons!

There's something about the sound of a dice roll tumbling across the table, as 4 friends all anxiously stare in suspense! The thrill of rolling a vital ability check, the suspense when the party barbarian tries to persuade an NPC, and the laughs that are had when the party rogue fumbles that stealth roll when sneaking into a nobles saferoom! It's the beauty of telling a story; finding out how heros are made, discovering why that villainous oathbreaker paladin abandoned his vows, and growing your character with your friends.
